Schools I Have Attended

>Oberlin College

I went to undergrad at Oberlin College. It is a small liberal arts school in Oberlin, OH. I studies astrophysics. My first year there the school changed its motto from "Think one person can change the world? We do." to "We are Oberlin. Fearless." The new one was terrible and by my senior year they changed it back. Probably the best part is that this is where I met my wife. Fun Fact: Oberlin has the highest percentage of its students marry other studies of any U.S. college or university.

>North Park Theological Seminary

I decided to pursue a career as a pastor. This led me to studying at North Park Theological Seminary in Chicago. I spent 4 years doing classes there, although I was primarily an online learner due to my wife's job elsewhere. I made some great friends and what I learned there has served me as I currently work as a pastor. There is a great restaurant next to campus called Merla's Kitchen which serves Filipino food that I loved going to when I was on campus.
